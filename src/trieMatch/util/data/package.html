(utility, data cleaning, symbology)    The util.data methods provide some general data support, as well processing for RIC and Tornado data.  The RIC methods provide enums and extraction for named-columns, repono and issuers.  The general methods support Americanization of Strings, tokenization, full and partial differences between column-groups of columnar data, extraction from variable-width columns, and quote-aware splitting of character-delimited data.
